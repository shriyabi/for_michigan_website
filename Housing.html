Housing Crisis in America

Rent

The Biden-Harris Administration knows rent is sky high and buying a home is out of reach for too many young people and hardworking Americans.

Biden-Harris are taking action to turn that around by:

  • ⇒ Sending a clear message to corporate landlords by capping rents at 5%.
  • ⇒ Repurposing public land to build more affordable homes.
  • ⇒ Investing $325 million to build thousands of homes across the country.

Monetary Relief




Fighting to take away valuable tax breaks from corporate landlords that raise rent by more than 5%, while also providing $10,000 in mortgage relief to unlock homeownership for young Americans looking to buy their first home.


Rental Assistance

Providing rental assistance to more than 5 million households, including an additional 100,000 low-income families, and called on Congress to extend assistance. Leading a crackdown on rental housing junk fees, including surprise "convenience fees." Implementing a Housing Supply Action Plan to build more housing across the country. Provided rental assistance to 8 million renters during the COVID-19 pandemic. Called on Congress to pass the Biden-Harris Housing Plan, which would build 2 million homes and lower costs for millions of young Americans.
