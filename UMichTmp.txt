                    <label for="ch12" style="color: #FFFFFF;"> X </label>
                    <p style="color: #FFFFFF;">
                        All registered voters in Michigan may cast their ballot at their assigned polling location on
                        August 6th (for the Primary Election) and November 5th (for the General Election). Polling
                        locations are open from 7 a.m. until 8 p.m. local time on Election Day. Voters in line by 8 p.m.
                        have the right to cast a ballot.
                        <br>
                    <div style="margin: 0.5em; background-color: #FFCB05;">
                        <p style="background-color: #FFCB05; color:#00274C;">Checklist: </p>
                        <ul>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> Make sure to go to the correct polling
                                    location! You can find your polling location at <a
                                        href="https://mvic.sos.state.mi.us/Voter/index" class = "link-color" style = "color:gray"> mi.gov/vote </a>.
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;">Triple-check your voter registration; you
                                    have until 8 p.m. on Election Day to register at your city or township clerk’s
                                    office (with proof of residency) if needed!
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> You do not need a photo ID to vote in
                                    Michigan, but bring a photo ID if you have one. It will make the process go faster.
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> Leave the killer campaign clothes and
                                    accessories at home!
                                </p>
                            </li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
                    </p>
                </div>
                <label for="ch12">
                    <h3 class="phone-text" style="color: white; align-self:flex-end;"> Voting In-Person </h3>
                </label>
            </div>
            <div class="boxes-other"
                style="background-color: #00274C; border-color: #FFCB05; background-image: url(VotingOpaque.png); background-size:cover; background-repeat: no-repeat; display: flex;">
                <input id="ch13" type="checkbox">
                <div class="content" style="background-color: #00274C;">
                    <label for="ch13" style="color: #FFFFFF;"> X </label>
                    <p style="color: #FFFFFF;">
                        Early voting works similarly to absentee voting. However, voters cast their ballot in person at
                        an early voting center – just like on election day. Any Michigan registered voter can now cast a
                        ballot in person up to 60 days before election day. The early voting period starts the second
                        Saturday before election day and ends the Sunday before.
                        <br>
                        <br>
                        <br>
                    <div style="margin: 0.5em; background-color: #FFCB05;">
                        <p style="background-color: #FFCB05; color:#00274C;">Checklist: </p>
                        <ul>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> Be registered to vote! </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;">Look up your early voting location within
                                    60 days of the election!
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> Visit your early voting location with
                                    photo identification and vote!
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> For more information, <a
                                        href="https://www.michigan.gov/sos/elections/voting/early-in-person-voting" class = "link-color" style = "color:gray">
                                        click here! </a></p>
                            </li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
                    </p>
                </div>
                <label for="ch13">
                    <h3 class="phone-text" style="color: white; align-self:flex-end;"> Voting Early </h3>
                </label>
            </div>
            <div class="boxes-other"
                style="background-color: #00274C; border-color: #FFCB05; background-image: url(AbsenteeOpaque.png); background-size:cover; background-repeat: no-repeat; display: flex;">
                <input id="ch14" type="checkbox">
                <div class="content" style="background-color: #00274C;">
                    <label for="ch14" style="color: #FFFFFF;"> X </label>
                    <p style="color: #FFFFFF;">
                        All registered Michigan voters can vote from home by absentee ballot starting 40 days before an
                        election. You can request an absentee ballot at any time, but we recommend applying at least 30
                        days before the election to allow ample mailing time. To return your ballot via mail, send the
                        ballot back at least two weeks before Election Day! (Ballot envelopes now come with pre-paid
                        postage, so no need to add a stamp.)
                        <br>
                    <div style="margin: 0.5em; background-color: #FFCB05;">
                        <p style="background-color: #FFCB05; color:#00274C;">Checklist: </p>
                        <ul>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> At least 30 days before Election Day, request an absentee ballot <a href = "https://mvic.sos.state.mi.us/AVApplication/Index" class = "link-color" style = "color:gray"> online </a>, <a href = "https://www.michigan.gov/sos/~/link.aspx?_id=08269D6FC8554AE388FED154A126B8B4&_z=z" class = "link-color" style = "color:gray"> by mail </a>, or <a href = "https://mvic.sos.state.mi.us/Voter/Index/#yourclerk" class = "link-color" style = "color:gray"> in person </a> at your city or township clerk’s office.
                                </p>
                                <li>
                                    <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> Make sure to mark the box on your application to join the permanent mail ballot list if you wish to receive a ballot automatically before every election without having to submit an application each time!
                                </p>
                                </li>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;">Fill out your absentee ballot. Don’t forget to sign and date the return envelope!
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> Return your absentee ballot by mail, to a ballot dropbox provided by your city or township clerk or in person to your local clerk’s office. For most voters, your absentee ballot must be received by 8 p.m. on Election Day. However, the ballots of voters serving in the military or living overseas must be postmarked by Election Day and received within six days of the election to be counted.
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> If returning your ballot by mail, please send it at least two weeks before Election Day.
                                </p>
                            </li>
                            <li>
                                <p style="text-align: left; color: #00274C;"> You can also bring your absentee ballot to your early voting site or (if you live anywhere but Detroit) your polling place on Election Day, check in with the poll workers, and insert your ballot directly into the tabulator.
                                </p>
                            </li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
                    </p>
