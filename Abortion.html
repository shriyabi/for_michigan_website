Abortion

    Advocacy and Leadership on Abortion Rights

  • Kamala Harris is the leading voice in the Biden Aministration to restore abortion rights. She strongly opposes the Supreme Court’s decision to overturn Roe v. Wade, allowing states to ban abortion. Harris called on Congress to restore the protections of Roe v. Wade in federal law.
    Harris helped organize a White House executive action to protect abortion and has campigned in 20 states calling on women to vote to protect their rights from government interference and called on Congress to pass a national law to protect abortion access. On her cross country protect reproductive freedom tour, she became the first Vice President to visit an abortion clinic.
  • Kamala is a better messenger on abortion than Biden is by 15 points. Among swing voters that gap is even larger.

Policy on Healthcare Access

    As Vice President, Kamala Harris established an Interagency Task Force on Reproductive Healthcare Access and issued strong guidance from HHS to ensure women are entitled to emergency care, even in states with extreme abortion bans. She defended the FDA's approval of safe and effective medication abortion and supported a woman's right to travel to another state for the care she needs. Harris also worked to strengthen access to affordable contraception, approved the first daily oral contraceptive for over-the-counter use, and supported access to fertility services. Additionally, she developed new resources through HHS and DOJ to provide timely and accurate information about reproductive health care and patients' rights, and strengthened privacy protections for patients and students.
